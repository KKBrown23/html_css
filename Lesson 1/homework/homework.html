This is my 6 step recipe for a Chocolate Brownie.

Below you can find the ingridients for this amazing dessert!

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Vanilla
  • Cocoa
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • And Chocolate!

In order to make this delicious dessert please follow the steps below:

  1. First get a baking pan and cover the inside wih chocolate.
  2. Melt your chocolate in a bowl and mix it.
  3. And your dry ingridients and mix them with the wet mix.
  4. You may add extra chocolate in the mix.
  5. Put the mix inside the baking ban and put it in the oven.
  6. How to know when they are done? Stick a toothpick in and hold for 2 seconds then take it out.
  7. If its super gooey then put it in the oven for another 25 minutes.
  8. If its moist and fudgy keep it in the oven for another 30 minutes.
  9. If its cakey and the tooth pick has a few crumbs left on it then keep it in the oven for another 30 minutes.
