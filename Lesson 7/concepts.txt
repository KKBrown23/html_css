Steps to position elements:
    1. Devide the elements into containers
    2. Display the container as flex
            display:flex;
    3. Use "justify-content" to move left-right
    4. Use "align-items" to move up-down

    5. "justify-content" and "align-items"

            flex-start
            center
            flex-end
            space-between 

When you say "display:flex"

    flex-direction:row
    align-items: strech
    
Carefull!!
    When you change the flex-direction:column 
        the "justify-content" will switch the job with "align-items"
